Welcome to the Setons page

Setons is a club at Gonzaga that consists of 30 girls in their sophomore year. Our three pillars that define our organization are service, sisterhood, and leadership. As a club, we meet once a week on Wednesday nights at 9 pm and noon on Sunday if you are on the Executive Board. Our goal is to foster meaningful relationships with eachother and the Spokane community. We foster these relationships through 2 hours of weekly community service in small groups, a full club service project each month, and a monthly social. My role in the club is Service Chair. As the service chair, I organize all of the weekly service placements and plan the monthly service events. Setons embodies themes that I am very passionate about and I am grateful for the opportunity to be a part of this amazing group.
