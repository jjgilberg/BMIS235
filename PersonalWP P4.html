Welcome to the Rowing page

The rowing team at Gonzaga is an underrated program, without a doubt. Our team has finished in the top 20 nationally over the past 5 years and is 9 time, consecutive, conference champions. The culture around the team and the coaching staff, makes this program truly unique.

Last year, I walked onto the Rowing team, having no prior experience. It was a tough learning curve, but I am grateful for the opportunity. As I learned how to row, set goals, and attack pieces, I learned a lot about myself and my approaches towards challenges in my life. I found a love for rowing and the sports I was involved in back in high school, faded away. This year, I've had the opportunity to compete in some of the top boats in our program and I couldn't be more thankful for the opportunity. Despite the early mornings and frequent soreness, I wouldn't change a thing.
